The North Carolina Broadband Infrastructure Office, with the Friday Institute at NC State University, is providing this quick speed test to gather data on internet connectivity in North Carolina. The data will be used to inform research, policy and funding recommendations to assist communities where internet access is inadequate.

Please allow your location to be accessed if a pop-up is presented in your browser.

Your location is critical to identifying areas without adequate internet service. Your exact location will be masked for privacy, but your general location may be:
  • mapped to show unmet needs or demand for high-speed internet
  • shared with relevant internet service providers or other partners to identify service solutions
  • included in a public map, with location points buffered for privacy
